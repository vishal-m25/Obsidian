### **1. Numbers & Arithmetic**  
- **LCM & HCF**  
  - $( \text{HCF} \times \text{LCM} = \text{Product of two numbers} )$
  - $( \text{LCM} = \frac{\text{Product of numbers}}{\text{HCF}} )$

- **Divisibility Rules**  
  - **2** → Last digit is even  
  - **3** → Sum of digits is divisible by 3  
  - **4** → Last two digits are divisible by 4  
  - **5** → Last digit is 0 or 5  
  - **6** → Divisible by both 2 and 3  
  - **7** → Double the last digit and subtract from remaining number  
  - **8** → Last three digits form a number divisible by 8  
  - **9** → Sum of digits is divisible by 9  
  - **11** → Difference between sum of alternate digits is 0 or divisible by 11

### **4. Profit & Loss**  
- **Profit** = $( \text{SP} - \text{CP} )$  
- **Loss** = $( \text{CP} - \text{SP} )$  
- **Profit %** = $( \frac{\text{Profit}}{\text{CP}} \times 100 )$

-  **SP (Selling Price) with Loss \%** = $( CP \times \frac{(100 - \text{Loss\%})}{100} )$  
- **Discount** = $( \frac{\text{Marked Price} - \text{Selling Price}}{\text{Marked Price}} \times 100 )$
